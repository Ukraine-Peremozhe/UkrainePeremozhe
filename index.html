Welcome to

Ukraine Peremozhe!

This websites goal is to teach you about Ukraine in the english language by providing you a large number of sources (Ukrainian sources are translated/subtitled). There are two main sections of this website: History and Culture. In the History section you will be given a summary of Ukrainian history by Ukraine Peremozhe, giving you the most important names and dates. You can look deeper into Ukrainian history by looking into the sources provided. In the Culture section you will enjoy exploring a beautiful culture, Ukrainian. There are also multiple areas you could explore in the extra information section. There is a "Contact Ukraine Peremozhe" box which you may use to ask questions, and if you want to learn about a specific topic, I will provide you with information. no matter how small, large, and however long it takes you to think of some questions, please use this form, because Ukraine Peremozhe will give you all answers so that they might, by chance, open you up into another world. Thank you for your attention, may you enjoy your time spent here. (This website was created for Kammerer Middle School as a school project).
