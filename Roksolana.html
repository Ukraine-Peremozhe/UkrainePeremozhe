Roksolana Virlan

Roksolana Virlan (Pohorilets) was born in Ukraine in the city of Lviv on July 18, 1971. In 1992, she graduated from the art college named after I. Trusha. In 2005, she left with her family for America. Lives and works in Louisville, Kentucky. A large number of works were written by the artist using the bas-relief technique in a three-dimensional style. Favorite themes of the author of the picture are devoted to philosophy, Ukrainian and Native American history. Every day is a new search for expression in art.

Dawn

"Dawn" - canvas, oil and other materials. Three-dimensional painting work. The idea of the work is to focus the viewer's attention on poppies. The poppy is a symbol of the spilled blood of Ukrainian heroes in the liberation war against Russia.

Cossack Mamay - Ukrainian Warrior

"Cossack Mamay - Ukrainian Warrior" - canvas, oil and other materials. Three-dimensional, bas-relief work. The content of the picture is to attract the public world to current events in Ukraine. The central figure of Mamay embodies the ancient symbolic image of the Cossack Mamay and the modern Ukrainian warrior. The deep inner strength of the Spirit, the calm but decisive look of the hero of the picture - this is the main philosophical component of the work. Equally important is the playing of the bandura, which tells us that a Cossack perceives life as a game - he is not afraid of death or the enemy, he perceives reality as part of the development of his inner world.

Full Moon

"Full Moon" - oil, canvas. The author of the work tried to convey the mood of a magical night during a full moon.

Native American Girl

"Native American Girl" - oil, canvas, auxiliary materials. Volumetric, three-dimensional painting work. A large and significant place in the work of Roksolana Virlan (Pohorilets) is occupied by the culture of Native Americans. The portrait of a Native American girl is a symbol of the fact that ancient nationalities live in the art of modern people and in their creativity and in our hearts.

Trypillian Melody

Picturesque three-dimensional work - "Trypillian Melody." Canvas, oil and other auxiliary materials. The author's idea is to reveal to the viewer the depth and symbolism of the ancient Trypillian culture, which was located on the territory of modern Ukraine.

Ukrainian Pysanky

Pysanky - voluminous, bas-reliefs three-dimensional work.. Oil, canvas and other materials. The art of Pysanky originates from a distant pre-Christian era. Each has a symbolic meaning.

Cossack Mamay

"Cossack Mamay" - canvas, oil. Mamay is a legendary character, a perfect warrior on a physical and spiritual level, who combines the best traits of human character: strength and nobility, free humanity, freedom and honor. Mamay personifies courage and observation, magical power and a philosopher. Mamay is a Ukrainian Cossack, who has been depicted in fine art for many centuries - he is a people's favorite and an educator.

Dance of Freedom

"Dance of Freedom" is a picturesque, voluminous miniature. Canvas, oil and other materials. This work represents the symbolism of Ukraine's fight for freedom in the Euromaidan of 2013-2014.

Tree of Pysanky

"Tree of Pysanky" - canvas, oil and other auxiliary materials. Volumetric, picturesque three-dimensional work. The idea of the work was to show how the ancient tradition and knowledge will continue and develop on the branches of the tree.

Motherhood

"Motherhood" is a painting created with oil on canvas. The idea of the painting is to convey the infinite depth of maternal love and self-sacrifice.

Angel

"Angel" - canvas, oil. The symbolic image of a playful angel in the picture gifts pure vibes and faith in goodness.

Paganini

"Paganini" - canvas, oil. Music in color, mood in forms. The strength of the spirit and the desire to create - that's what this picture conveys.

Magical Forest

"Magical Forest" is a three-dimensional painting work. Oil, canvas and other materials.

Lunar Melody

"Lunar Melody" - oil on canvas. The author of the work sought to convey her own understanding of sound and vibration and the mystery of the night.

Moonlit Forest

"Moonlit Forest" - oil, canvas, three-dimensional execution. The power of mysticism and sounds of the night in colors and forms.

Summer

"Summer" canvas, oil three-dimensional execution.

Girl in the Flowers

"Girl in the Flowers" - symbol of spring, beauty and the development of life. Canvas, oil, three-dimensional presentation of painting.

Cossack Chaika

"Cossack Chaika" - canvas, oil, three-dimensional execution. The painting symbolizes the eternal struggle in life, the strength of spirit of Ukrainian Cossack heroes and the ability to steer a symbolic boat in a storm. The Chaika is a historic boat used by the Ukrainian Cossacks.

Rain in Lviv

"Rain in Lviv" is a voluminous, three-dimensional work. Canvas, oil and other auxiliary materials. Lviv is the native city of the author of the work, which is already 767 years old from the time of its foundation by King Danylo of Halychyna. Lviv is a city with a long and complex history, a city of romantic meetings and the birth of great people of the Ukrainian people - musicians, artists, scientists, poets and writers. Lviv is a city that cannot be forgotten, because its impressive architecture makes it cozy and warm.
