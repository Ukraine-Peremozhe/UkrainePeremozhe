Ukrainian Folk Songs

Ukrainian folk songs are a beauty of Ukrainian culture, with Ukraine being the nation with the most documented folk songs in the world. Ukrainian folk songs are known for their melancholy and bittersweet melodies. Most Ukrainian folk songs were written during the time of the Cossacks, but in this section, Ukrainian folk songs up to the 20th century will be included. Cossacks were constantly at war for freedom. During times of war, Cossacks carried three important items; sword, pipe, and an instrument. Cossacks would sing before and after war because they would have nothing else to do while sitting next to their dead brothers who fought for freedom. Cossack music truly has a soul; when you hear it, you know it is Ukrainian, and it is this way because of its history.
